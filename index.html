Transportation

We are using school fees, government funding, and private investment for school and college transportation. We have 75 institutions, so we could average 2-3 buses for each institution.

For hospitals and clinics, there will be about 10-15 ambulances for the whole city.

The budget for both institutional and clinical transport is 600 crores.

We have enough agricultural land where the produced goods are grown and shipped to other required towns (for trade/sale). So, transportation is provided to move goods to other cities, including neighboring areas.

Additionally, we have a water treatment plant in our city where water can be processed, and can be transported to neighbor cities/towns.

We also need to bring goods that we can't produce in our city, so we will import them from other places.

Our city is known for its major crop production. To transport goods, we plan to use heavy trucks.

The budget for this is 4,000 crores.

For local transportation, we will allow auto rickshaws and mini vans, where they are taxed appropriately.

In our city, there is a metro system that covers most of the main roads, and the budget for this is 50,000 crores.

Since many people never own a vehicle, they can use the metro for transportation.

MNCs can also, for their own purposes, have their own transportation to get raw materials to their factories and transport the produced materials. These are taxpayers (Transportation Association).

There is a railway station where people can travel to many other cities and towns.

We have requested the central government for a train route to include our city.

There are also government buses to many other cities, running at regular intervals based on demand and distance.

The total budget for institutional, clinical, government buses, and the railway station is 5000 crores.
