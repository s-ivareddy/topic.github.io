Water Infrastructure:-

  • Install rainwater harvesting systems in all buildings for non-potable use such as irrigation and toilet flushing.
  • Develop large-scale community rainwater harvesting facilities for industrial and agricultural use.
  • Build centralized water treatment plants for recycling wastewater and using treated water for landscaping and cooling.
  • Expand water recycling systems for residential and industrial zones.
  • Deploy smart sensors in water pipelines to detect leaks and optimize water usage.
  • budget:-2500 crores

Waste Management:-

  • Introduce waste segregation systems in all homes and businesses, with bins for organic, recyclable, and non-recyclable waste.
  • Set up community composting systems to process organic waste locally.
  • Establish waste-to-energy plants to convert non-recyclable waste into electricity.
  • Build recycling centers for paper, plastic, glass, and metal, with public awareness programs.
  • Launch up cycling programs to repurpose non-recyclable materials into new products.
  • Design and construct all new buildings to meet green building standards, incorporating energy-efficient features such as passive cooling, insulation, and solar panels.
  • Develop mixed-use, mixed-income neighborhoods to reduce commute times and promote local living.
  • Implement water-efficient building standards with low-flow fixtures, dual-flush toilets, and greywater systems.
  • budget:-1000 crores

Community Infrastructure:-

  • Build energy-efficient schools, clinics, and community centers designed with renewable energy, natural lighting, and green roofs.
  • Establish vocational training centers for green jobs in renewable energy, sustainable agriculture, and green building techniques.
  • Provide healthcare services with eco-friendly systems and green building certifications.
