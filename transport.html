Infrastructure

Institutional Transportation:-

  • Funding Sources: School fees, government funding, and private investments.
  • Deployment: 75 institutions averaging 2-3 buses each for school and college transportation.
  • Budget: Allocated within a 600-crore fund shared with clinical transportation.

Clinical Transportation:-

  • Coverage: 10-15 ambulances designated for the entire city.
  • Budget: Shared with institutional transport, totaling 600 crores.

  • Imports: Essential goods not produced locally brought in from external sources.
  • Infrastructure: Heavy trucks utilized for bulk transport.
  • Budget: Dedicated 4,000 crores for agricultural and trade-related transportation.

Local Transportation:-

  • Vehicles: Auto-rickshaws and mini-vans regulated via appropriate taxes.
  • Metro System: Covers major roads within the city, offering affordable transportation for the vehicle-less population.
  • Budget: Metro system funded with 50,000 crores.

Corporate and Industrial Transport:-

  • Multinational Companies: Own transport systems for sourcing raw materials and distributing products.
  • Taxation: MNC transportation subject to taxation under the Transportation Association.

Inter-City and Long-Distance Transport:-

  • Railway Station: Serves connectivity to various cities and towns.
  • Central Government Collaboration: Proposed inclusion in a national train route.
  • Government Buses: Regular services connecting to other cities, adjusted based on demand and distance.
  • Budget: 5,000 crores allocated for railway services, government buses, and institutional/clinical transport.
